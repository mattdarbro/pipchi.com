Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All app data is stored locally on your device. Some apps offer optional iCloud sync, which stores data in your personal iCloud account. We never store your data on our servers.

How do I delete my data?

Simply delete the app from your device to remove all local data. If you use iCloud sync, you can manage that data through your Apple ID settings.

Do your apps work offline?

Most features work offline. Local Poet and Prompt Script require an internet connection for their AI features. The Narrows is unusual: it's an AI chat app that runs entirely on-device using Apple's Foundation Models, so it works without any internet connection at all. Eraser Adventures, Quiet Appreciation, Mixtape Pinball, and The Narrows all work fully offline.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please email us with a description of the issue, which app and device you're using, and any screenshots that might help. We read every message and fix issues as quickly as we can.
